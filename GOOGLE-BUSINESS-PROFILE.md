## 🤝 Customer Interaction Settings

Once your profile is active, enable the following features in your dashboard:
*   **Enable Customer Reviews:** Send your short link to satisfied customers to build your reputation.
*   **Enable Messaging:** Allow customers to chat with you directly from Google Search and Maps.
*   **Respond to Reviews:** Professionally reply to all reviews (positive and negative) to show you value customer feedback.

## ✅ Verification & Google Maps

To ensure your profile appears in **Google Maps search results**:
1.  Sign in to [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/).
2.  Click **Manage now** and follow the prompts using the exact details above.
3.  **Verify your business:** Google typically requires verification via a phone code (SMS/Call), video recording, or sometimes a postcard. Follow their prompted method to get the verified checkmark.
4.  Once verified, your business will seamlessly appear across Google Search and Google Maps!
